01

The manifesto

Focus isn't a feature you toggle. It's a position you take. Lock In is the door you close on the noise — a single, deliberate session where the only move left is the work in front of you.

Most apps fracture your attention and call it productivity. We do the opposite: one timer, one intention, zero feeds, zero badges. The interface disappears the moment you begin.

When the session ends, it stays ended. No streak guilt, no dopamine loop — just a clean record of the hours you actually spent locked in.

02

Three pillars

01 — Sessions

The ritual timer

Set an intention, choose a length, and start. The screen goes quiet and the clock takes over. One session at a time — the way deep work actually happens.

02 — Record

Hours, not streaks

A calm ledger of the time you've truly focused. No guilt mechanics, no broken-streak shame — just honest signal you can look back on.

03 — Silence

Block the noise

Notifications, apps, and the urge to check — all held at the door for the length of your session. Come back when you're done, not before.
